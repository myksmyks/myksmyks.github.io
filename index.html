About the Photographer

Welcome to Equine Moments

With over 15 years of experience in equine photography, I specialize in capturing the unique bond between horses and their human companions. My passion for these magnificent creatures began in childhood and has evolved into a professional calling.

From intimate portrait sessions to dynamic action shots at competitions, I bring technical expertise and artistic vision to every shoot. My goal is to create timeless images that showcase the beauty, power, and spirit of horses.

Based in the heart of horse country, I travel throughout the region working with professional riders, trainers, and horse enthusiasts who want to preserve their most precious memories.
